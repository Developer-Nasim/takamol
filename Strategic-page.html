Strategic Alignment

Definition: This domain focuses on how well the Project Management Office (PMO) aligns its projects and initiatives with the strategic goals and objectives of the organization. It ensures that the PMO's efforts are directly contributing to the company's long-term vision and mission.

To what extent do our projects align with the organization's strategic objectives?* 

The purpose of this question is to: Evaluates how projects support the broader goals of the organization, ensuring that project efforts contribute to the overall strategic direction.
